About the Club

The club collaborates on projects and encourages technical skill-growth.

Club meetings revolve around a mix of collaborative project work and LeetCode/DailyCodingProblem whiteboarding.

Current club projects are: A Discord server bot, this splash page, Hex-e robot construction, and Cue robot programming.
