About the Club

We are the Computer Science Club of Central Washington University (2022 - 2024).

Our club is open to all students and alumni of Central Washington University, people are welcome to join from any major.

The club collaborates on projects and encourages professional & technical skill-growth.

Club meetings revolve around a mix of collaborative projects, LeetCode problem whiteboarding, and technical workshops.

Current club projects are: This splash webpage, Hex-e robot construction, Cue robot programming, Custom B-Mo robot designing, and a Discord Bot integrated with ChatGPT.

Past workshops have been on Resume and Cover Letter Writing, Technical Job Application Processes, Technical Career Specialization Pathways, AI Generative Tooling, and Website Decvelopment.
